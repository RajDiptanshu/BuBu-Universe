# Character Bible: Bubu the Baby Elephant

Bubu is the **sole, permanent mascot** of the channel. Every episode, every pillar, every thumbnail features Bubu rendered to this exact specification. This document is the single source of truth for AI image/video generation prompts — any drift from these rules breaks brand consistency and silhouette recognition.

---

## Final Mascot

- **Name:** Bubu
- **Species:** Baby elephant (original character design — generic/iconic, not based on any existing show or character)
- **Role:** Sole on-screen host across the entire channel. Bubu discovers, reacts to, and triggers the payoff in every episode.

---

## Proportions

| Element | Ratio of total height |
|---|---|
| Head | ~50% |
| Body | ~35% |
| Legs | ~15% |
| Trunk (at rest) | Contained within head silhouette (curled) |
| Trunk (active/extended) | Extends 25–30% of body height beyond the body envelope |

Bubu is drawn at **"2 heads tall"** — an extreme baby-proportion ratio that reads instantly as "infant" and maximizes the baby-schema cuteness response.

---

## Body Shape

- Single soft **egg/rounded-oval torso**, no visible neck — head sits directly on body.
- Four short, stubby legs, rendered as simple rounded cylinders/ovals. In the default 3/4-front pose, typically only 2–3 legs are visible.
- No tail emphasis (tail exists but is small and not a primary expressive element — unlike trunk/ears).
- Overall silhouette: one large rounded head-shape with two large ear-circles on top, sitting on a smaller rounded body. **No other shape in the design should compete with this silhouette.**

---

## Ear Shape

- Two large, **near-circular ears**, each approximately **35–40% of head width**, attached symmetrically high on the head.
- Ears are the **#1 silhouette signature** — combined with the trunk, they make Bubu identifiable as a flat black shape with zero internal detail.
- Ears are mostly static (do not flap constantly) — they move only as an **expression signal** (see Expression Set), which keeps their motion meaningful rather than distracting.
- Ear color = body color on the outside; pink inner-ear color visible only when ears are in the "Surprise" or "Delight" positions.

---

## Trunk Rules

The trunk is Bubu's primary "hand" and the main driver of cause-and-effect payoffs.

1. **At rest:** short, curls into a simple "J" shape near the mouth — contained mostly within the head silhouette.
2. **Active:** extends as **one continuous, single-curve tube** with a rounded tip — never more than one bend, never segmented or "wiggly."
3. **Function set (only these 4 actions, reused across all episodes):**
   - **Point** — trunk extends straight toward an object
   - **Hold** — trunk curls around an object, tip wraps once
   - **Spray** — trunk lifts, tip opens slightly, payoff particles (water/confetti/bubbles) emit from tip
   - **Pat/Tap** — trunk taps an object 1–3 times (used for rhythmic Build beats)
4. **Trunk never** interacts with more than one object at a time, never splits, never shows fingers/segments.

---

## Facial Features

- **Eyes:** large, round, each ≈ 22–25% of face width, positioned lower-middle of the face, close together (≈1 eye-width gap). Dark fill (`#2B2B2B`) with one fixed white highlight dot — never animated reflections, to keep generation consistent.
- **Eyebrows:** simple single-stroke arcs above each eye — the primary expression-change element along with the trunk/ears.
- **Mouth:** small, simple closed-curve smile by default. Opens only for "Surprise" (small "o") — never shown wide open or with visible teeth/tongue.
- **No tusks.** (Avoids "adult elephant" read and reduces silhouette complexity.)
- **No wrinkles, no skin texture, no toenails.** Flat, smooth shading throughout — every surface is a clean gradient or flat color.

## Fixed Color Palette

| Part | Color | Hex |
|---|---|---|
| Body skin | Soft gray-blue | `#A9B7C4` |
| Belly / face patch | Pale cream | `#EAEFF2` |
| Inner ears | Soft pink | `#F4B8C0` |
| Cheeks (blush) | Pink | `#FBC7C7` |
| Eyes | Near-black + white highlight | `#2B2B2B` / `#FFFFFF` |
| Trunk tip (subtle shading only) | Slightly darker gray-blue | `#8FA0AE` |
| Accessory (rotates — see below) | Episode hero color | Variable |

This palette is **never altered**. It is the single biggest lever for instant brand/character recognition across 100+ episodes.

---

## Expression Set (exactly 4 — no others used, ever)

| # | Name | Eyes/Brows | Ears | Trunk | Used in beat |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Curious** (default) | Wide, soft brows raised slightly | Relaxed, forward-facing | Gentle "J" curl, slight sway | Hello / Approach |
| 2 | **Focused** | One brow slightly lowered | Still | Extended in Point or Pat | Build |
| 3 | **Surprise** | Brows raised high, eyes wide | Pop fully outward (pink interior visible) | Lifts straight up | Anticipation |
| 4 | **Delight** | Eyes scrunch into crescents | Flap once, pink interior flashes | Raised overhead in Spray/trumpet pose | Payoff |

---

## Accessory Rules

- Bubu may wear **exactly one** small accessory per episode: a thin neck bandana OR a small round ear-charm.
- Accessory color = the episode's **hero color** (ties Bubu visually to the lesson without changing his fixed palette).
- The accessory must **never** cover the eyes, trunk, or ears, and must **never** change Bubu's silhouette shape.
- No accessory may be worn in two different styles within the same episode (one accessory, one color, for the full runtime).

---

## Do / Don't Rules

**DO:**
- Always render both ears as large, symmetric, near-circles.
- Always render the trunk as a single smooth curve (one bend maximum when active).
- Always use the exact fixed hex palette above.
- Always keep Bubu's face toward camera for the majority of each episode (front or 3/4-front).
- Always use only the 4 defined expressions.
- Always keep Bubu at "2 heads tall" proportions.

**DON'T:**
- Don't add tusks, tusks-shadows, wrinkles, texture detail, or toenails.
- Don't change ear size, shape, or placement between episodes.
- Don't show the trunk segmented, multi-jointed, or interacting with 2+ objects at once.
- Don't introduce a second character that overlaps or merges with Bubu's silhouette.
- Don't change Bubu's body color, even for "night" or "underwater" scenes (adjust background/lighting instead).
- Don't add clothing beyond the single rotating accessory.
- Don't hold Bubu in full back-view for more than a brief transitional moment — the face/silhouette must remain the focal point.
