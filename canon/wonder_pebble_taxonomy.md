# Wonder Pebble Taxonomy

**Purpose:** This is the master registry of every **Flagship Wonder Pebble** in Bubu's world — the small, curated set of named, collectible pebbles that anchor the franchise's continuity system (`canon/bubu_universe_bible.md` §4 and §10). It exists so that 100+ episodes, future songs, books, toys, and learning content can all draw from one consistent, non-contradictory set of "named treasures," without any single episode needing to invent one ad hoc.

**How to read this document:**

- **Every episode** features **one Wonder Pebble**, which flashes the franchise's fixed turquoise (`#4FD1C5`) at the Anticipation→Payoff transition — this never changes, named or not (`bubu_universe_bible.md` §4).
- **Most episodes** use a **Generic Wonder Pebble** — pale gray-blue, no entry in this document, resets per episode, and is never referenced again.
- **A small number of episodes** (this document's 25 entries) use a **Flagship Wonder Pebble** — the same in-episode turquoise flash, but once claimed by an episode, it is permanently added to Pebble Hill in its **facet color** (a secondary color, visible *only* at Pebble Hill, never in-episode) and never reused.
- **"Color" below always means the facet color** — the Pebble Hill identity used for merchandise, collectibles, and continuity bookkeeping. It is never shown during the episode itself.

## Category Overview

| Category | Count | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **Franchise Flagship** | 5 | Anchor major franchise "firsts" — establish the Wonder Pebble system itself, the Nook/home, Animal Friends, and the franchise's signature "favorite thing" (water/splash). |
| **Educational** | 12 | One per major teaching concept across the 8 content pillars — colors, shapes, counting, vehicles, playground, routines, bilingual (Hindi) vocabulary. |
| **Seasonal** | 4 | Real-world calendar tie-ins (New Year, monsoon, festival season, winter/cozy season) — facet-color and merchandising hooks only; **the in-world meadow never changes with seasons** (per World Rule #7's "always calm, always daylight"). |
| **Rare** | 4 | Milestone and reserved pebbles — Episode 50, Episode 100, a future Big Feelings flagship, and a long-form compilation recap pebble. |

## A. Franchise Flagship Pebbles (5)

### 1. First Wonder Pebble
- **Color:** Deep Cyan
- **Learning objective:** Introduces the Wonder Pebble mechanic itself — "small gray things can hide something wonderful."
- **Discovery trigger:** The very first pale gray-blue pebble shown using the new mechanic, visible from Scene 1.
- **Typical payoff:** Whatever pillar Episode 02 uses, plus an extra emphasis frame establishing Pebble Hill's first turquoise stone.
- **Action words:** Wow! (its first-ever appearance as the discovery word)
- **Emotional outcome:** Foundational wonder — "this is where it all started."
- **Merchandising potential:** "Episode 02 / Pebble #1" commemorative item; app onboarding reward for new viewers.
- **Claimed by:** Episode 02.

### 2. Splash Pebble
- **Color:** Aqua Blue
- **Learning objective:** Reinforces cause-and-effect + sensory vocabulary ("wet," "cool") — Bubu's established favorite thing (`bubu_personality_bible.md` #4).
- **Discovery trigger:** A second Water Play episode's puddle, post-Episode 01.
- **Typical payoff:** Trunk-spray bursts the puddle into a turquoise splash.
- **Action words:** Tap! / Splash! / Wow!
- **Emotional outcome:** Pure joy — the franchise's signature "favorite thing" moment, now formally part of Pebble Hill.
- **Merchandising potential:** Flagship plush/keychain bundle; bath-toy line anchor.
- **Claimed by:** The first Episode 02+ Water Play episode.

### 3. First Friend Pebble
- **Color:** Sunshine Yellow
- **Learning objective:** Social concept "friend" / greeting.
- **Discovery trigger:** A new Animal Friend's arrival at Friendship Pond.
- **Typical payoff:** The friend-animal performs its signature movement toward Bubu; both react in Delight.
- **Action words:** Hop! / Wow!
- **Emotional outcome:** Warmth, belonging — the franchise's first new "face."
- **Merchandising potential:** First Animal Friends companion toy/plush set.
- **Claimed by:** The episode that opens Friendship Pond (Animal Friends pillar debut).

### 4. Counting Crown Pebble
- **Color:** Golden Amber
- **Learning objective:** Numbers one through three; the concept "three" as a complete set.
- **Discovery trigger:** The third object placed at the Counting Tree completes the count.
- **Typical payoff:** All three objects glow and bounce in sync.
- **Action words:** Tap! / Pop! / Wow!
- **Emotional outcome:** Pride and mastery — "I counted all of them!"
- **Merchandising potential:** Counting blocks / app counter tie-in.
- **Claimed by:** The first Counting Tree episode that completes a full 1-2-3 sequence.

### 5. Home Glow Pebble
- **Color:** Warm Coral
- **Learning objective:** Routine concept "all done"; comfort and self-regulation.
- **Discovery trigger:** Completing a Daily Routine's third repetition at the Nook.
- **Typical payoff:** A soft glow and a contented sigh — the franchise's calmest payoff.
- **Action words:** Tap! / Wow! / Done!
- **Emotional outcome:** Calm contentment — the Nook's first "named" treasure.
- **Merchandising potential:** Bedtime nightlight / glow-feature plush.
- **Claimed by:** The first Daily Routines episode set at the Nook.

## C. Seasonal Pebbles (4)

Seasonal pebbles are **facet-color and merchandising hooks only** — they are produced and released during a real-world calendar window, but the in-episode meadow, lighting, and weather **never change** (World Rule #7: always calm, always daylight, no day/night or weather cycle). The "season" exists in marketing metadata and Pebble Hill's facet color, not in the frame.

### 18. New Beginnings Pebble
- **Color:** Pale Gold | **Real-world window:** New Year (January) | **Learning:** Concept "new" | **Discovery trigger:** A standard Generic-pebble discovery, released during the New Year window. | **Payoff:** Standard payoff for its pillar; Pebble Hill cameo at the Loop beat is slightly brighter than usual. | **Action words:** Wow! / New! | **Emotional outcome:** Hope, freshness. | **Merch potential:** New Year sticker pack / limited-time app badge.

### 19. Festival Lights Pebble
- **Color:** Marigold Gold/Orange | **Real-world window:** Festival-of-lights season | **Learning:** Color "gold" / concept "light" | **Discovery trigger:** A Color Garden episode released during the festival window. | **Payoff:** The object fills marigold-gold and glows softly — **no flashing or strobing**, staying within the calm-brand saturation limits (`canon/visual_style_guide.md`). | **Action words:** Tap! / Glow! / Wow! | **Emotional outcome:** Festive warmth — culturally resonant without requiring cultural context to enjoy (mirrors the Wobble/"Arre, Bubu!" approach). | **Merch potential:** Festival-edition plush/sticker, regional marketing push.

### 20. Rainy Day Pebble
- **Color:** Storm Blue-Gray | **Real-world window:** Monsoon season | **Learning:** Gentle weather-adjacent vocabulary ("rain," "drip"), handled via the existing Whisper Grass "distant rumble → calm" mechanic | **Discovery trigger:** A Whisper Grass / Big Feelings episode released during the monsoon window. | **Payoff:** The standard fear→calm resolution (`bubu_personality_bible.md` #5), ending in a soft glow. | **Action words:** Wow! / Calm! | **Emotional outcome:** Comfort, safety modeled explicitly. | **Merch potential:** Comfort-plush tie-in for the Big Feelings pillar.

### 21. Snug Season Pebble
- **Color:** Soft Cream | **Real-world window:** Winter / cozy holiday season | **Learning:** Routine concept "cozy" / "all done" | **Discovery trigger:** A Nook/Daily Routines episode released during the winter window. | **Payoff:** An extra-soft glow and contented sigh — the gentlest Daily Routines payoff. | **Action words:** Wow! / Snug! | **Emotional outcome:** Coziness. | **Merch potential:** Blanket/plush bundle tie-in.

## D. Rare Pebbles (4)

### 22. Fifty Wonders Pebble
- **Color:** Platinum Silver
- **Tie-in:** Episode 50 milestone.
- **Learning objective:** Celebration/review — no new concept introduced.
- **Discovery trigger:** Pebble Hill itself is the episode's focus, visibly transitioning from "Moderate" to "Dense" tier (World Rule #8, `bubu_universe_bible.md` §10).
- **Typical payoff:** The widest Pebble Hill reveal shown to date.
- **Action words:** Wow! (only — no build/payoff word pair)
- **Emotional outcome:** Collective pride, milestone recognition.
- **Merchandising potential:** "50 Episodes" collector pin / certificate; premium licensing moment.

### 23. Hundred Wonders Pebble
- **Color:** Rose Gold
- **Tie-in:** Episode 100 milestone.
- **Learning objective:** Celebration/review — no new concept introduced.
- **Discovery trigger:** Pebble Hill reaches full "Dense/field" tier on screen for the first time.
- **Typical payoff:** The franchise's widest-ever Pebble Hill reveal.
- **Action words:** Wow! (only)
- **Emotional outcome:** Awe at scale and history.
- **Merchandising potential:** Flagship anniversary toy line.

### 24. Quiet Pebble
- **Color:** Pale Lilac
- **Tie-in:** Reserved for a future Big Feelings / Whisper Grass flagship episode (post-Episode 25, not yet scheduled).
- **Learning objective:** Emotional regulation — calm after a startle.
- **Discovery trigger:** Appears only *after* Bubu's fear response (`bubu_personality_bible.md` #5) has fully resolved — the only Wonder Pebble whose discovery is contingent on a prior emotional beat, not a Build repetition.
- **Typical payoff:** The gentlest payoff in the franchise — a slow, soft glow with no music swell, carried only by the Wonder Pebble discovery twinkle (`canon/bubu_audio_bible.md`).
- **Action words:** Wow! / Calm!
- **Emotional outcome:** Relief, reassurance.
- **Merchandising potential:** Sleep-aid / nightlight tie-in; parent-facing wellness marketing angle.

### 25. Rainbow Pebble
- **Color:** Multi-facet — shifts through every hero color used so far, visible only at Pebble Hill.
- **Tie-in:** Reserved, long-form-only — claimed at a major compilation milestone (e.g., an "Episodes 1-50" compilation), not a standalone Short.
- **Learning objective:** Synthesis/review of prior lessons — no single new concept.
- **Discovery trigger:** The compilation's finale; Pebble Hill briefly shimmers through the colors of every Flagship pebble claimed so far.
- **Typical payoff:** A wide Pebble Hill shot showing the full collected history, ending on the Rainbow Pebble itself.
- **Action words:** Wow! (only)
- **Emotional outcome:** Wonder plus nostalgia — recognition of the franchise's own history.
- **Merchandising potential:** Premium "collector's edition" multi-color plush/sticker pack — the franchise's top licensing item.

## Future Franchise Roadmap (Episode 26+)

- **Episodes 26-49:** Continue Educational/Seasonal pebble releases at a similar pace (roughly 1 named pebble per 3-5 episodes), drawn from remaining pillar/concept combinations not yet covered (e.g., additional colors, shapes, vehicle types, Animal Friends), with new entries added to this taxonomy as they're designed — never retrofitted onto already-published episodes.
- **Episode 50:** **Fifty Wonders Pebble** — Pebble Hill's Sparse→Moderate→Dense progression (`bubu_universe_bible.md` §10) becomes visibly "Dense" for the first time.
- **Episode 100:** **Hundred Wonders Pebble** — Pebble Hill reaches its fullest on-screen presentation.
- **Reserved, unscheduled:** **Quiet Pebble** (next Big Feelings flagship episode) and **Rainbow Pebble** (next major compilation) — both claimed only when their triggering content is actually produced, never pre-assigned to a episode number.
- **Toy/book/app integration:** Once 10+ Flagship pebbles are claimed (expected around Episode 20-25), Pebble Hill itself becomes viable as a standalone collectible "map" — a physical play-set or app screen showing every claimed pebble in its facet color — without requiring any change to in-episode visuals or mechanics.
