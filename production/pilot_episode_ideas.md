## First 10 Production Batch (Easiest-to-Generate Set)

To make the **first 10 episodes extremely easy to generate** (single static object, one-transform payoff, no second character, no fast motion, default world background), the recommended starting batch is:

1. Red Balloon Pop (Colors)
2. Circle Rolls Away (Shapes)
3. Bubu Counts Two Leaves (Counting)
4. Yellow Sun Wakes Up (Colors)
5. Star Shines (Shapes)
6. Green Leaf Grows (Colors)
7. Purple Berry Surprise (Colors)
8. Blue Water Drop (Water Play / Colors)
9. Three Bubbles Float (Counting)
10. Square Block Stack (Shapes)

These 10 use only: default meadow world, 1–2 secondary objects, simple fill/glow/roll/float payoffs, and no second characters or fast motion-streaks — the lowest-risk combination for early AI-video generation passes while still covering 3 of the 8 pillars (Colors, Shapes, Counting) plus an early taste of Water Play.

**Note on Episode 1 selection:** Although "Bubu's Splash" (Water Play) is rated Medium rather than Easy, it is recommended as the **flagship Episode 1** (see `episode_01_full_package.md`) because it establishes Bubu's signature trunk-spray action — the single most brand-defining, highest-retention payoff — right from the start. The Easy-tier batch above then forms the low-risk ramp for episodes 2–11.
