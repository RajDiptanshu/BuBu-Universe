Vertical 9:16, Bubu 40–55% of frame height, eyes ~40% from top, center-left. No tusks, no wrinkles, no extra characters, no text, no watermark.

2. **Build (Focused):** Bubu leaning slightly forward, trunk rotated ~90° with its tip resting on the left focus pebble in a gentle "pat" pose; both focus pebbles unchanged (still pale); background pebbles unchanged.
3. **Anticipation (Surprise):** Bubu upright and still, trunk rotated ~180° straight up, ears popped outward; both focus pebbles still pale, perfectly motionless; background pebbles unchanged.
4. **Payoff (Delight):** Bubu mid-hop, ears flapped outward, trunk rotated ~215–225° up and back toward the near ear; both focus pebbles now fully saturated yellow (`#FFE066`, ~0.85 sat) — the two brightest elements in frame; background turquoise pebbles glow at their established softer intensity.
5. **Loop (Curious, matches Scene 1):** Bubu back in its Scene-1 resting pose and trunk position (0°); both focus pebbles retain their yellow glow (slightly softer than peak Payoff); background pebbles unchanged — four glowing pebbles now visible on the Hill.

## 3. Video Prompts

Static, locked-off 9:16 camera throughout — no pans, tilts, zooms, or cuts. Image-to-video from each scene's keyframe; motion only.

1. **Hello (2s):** Gentle idle Wobble; the two background pebbles glow with a slow, soft steady pulse. In the final frames, Bubu's body begins leaning forward and the trunk begins lowering toward the left focus pebble.
2. **Build (4s):** The trunk pats the left pebble, then the right pebble, then the left pebble again — three pats total, evenly spaced (~1.3s each), each pat identical in speed and size, no ripple or growth. The background pebbles remain a steady soft glow. After the third pat, the trunk lifts and the body straightens.
3. **Anticipation (2s):** Near-freeze. Both focus pebbles remain perfectly still and pale. Bubu holds upright, trunk straight up, ears popped outward; only a slow blink and faint breathing motion.
4. **Payoff (3s):** In the first ~0.5s both focus pebbles rapidly brighten from pale gray-blue to fully saturated yellow at the same instant and hold that glow. Simultaneously Bubu hops once, ears flap outward, trunk swings up and back. The background turquoise pebbles' glow stays at its established intensity, unchanged. Over the remaining ~2.5s everything settles.
5. **Loop (1s, generated at 2s minimum then trimmed):** Bubu eases from the Delight hop back into its calm Scene-1 resting pose and trunk curl; both focus pebbles settle to a soft steady yellow glow alongside the two background turquoise pebbles. End frame should closely match Scene 1's opening frame except for the two newly-glowing focus pebbles.
