## 3. Video Prompts

Static, locked-off 9:16 camera throughout — no pans, tilts, zooms, or cuts. Image-to-video from each scene's keyframe; motion only.

1. **Hello (2s):** Gentle idle Wobble; the background pebble glows with a slow, soft steady pulse. In the final frames, Bubu's body begins leaning forward and the trunk begins lowering toward the focus pebble.
2. **Build (4s):** The trunk gently pats the focus pebble three times, evenly spaced (~1.3s each), each pat identical in speed and size — no ripple, no growth, just a calm repeated touch. The background pebble remains a steady soft glow. After the third pat, the trunk lifts and the body straightens.
3. **Anticipation (2s):** Near-freeze. The focus pebble remains perfectly still and pale. Bubu holds upright, trunk straight up, ears popped outward; only a slow blink and faint breathing motion.
4. **Payoff (3s):** In the first ~0.5s the focus pebble rapidly brightens from pale gray-blue to fully saturated turquoise and holds that glow. Simultaneously Bubu hops once, ears flap outward, trunk swings up and back. The background pebble's glow stays at its established intensity, unchanged. Over the remaining ~2.5s everything settles.
5. **Loop (1s, generated at 2s minimum then trimmed):** Bubu eases from the Delight hop back into its calm Scene-1 resting pose and trunk curl; the focus pebble settles to a soft steady glow alongside the background pebble. End frame should closely match Scene 1's opening frame except for the newly-glowing focus pebble.

## 5. Educational Objective

Introduces the number word "One" by presenting a single object in the object zone and rewarding sustained, gentle attention to it — linking the number concept directly to the Wonder Pebble payoff rather than to an abstract counting exercise.

## 6. Brand-Building Objective

Begins the visible "Pebble Hill grows" arc — by the end of this episode, two pebbles glow on the Hill instead of one, giving repeat viewers their first concrete evidence that the world remembers across episodes (World Rule #8), ahead of the larger "grows" milestones in Episodes 08, 17, and beyond.
