## 3. Video Prompts

Static, locked-off 9:16 camera throughout — no pans, tilts, zooms, or cuts. Image-to-video from each scene's keyframe; motion only.

1. **Hello (2s):** Gentle idle Wobble. In the final frames, Bubu's body begins leaning forward and the trunk begins lifting toward the focus pebble.
2. **Build (4s):** Bubu's trunk performs three repeated forward "point" jabs toward the focus pebble, evenly spaced (~1.3s each), pausing briefly between each. The pebbles remain completely still throughout. After the third point, the trunk lifts toward vertical, body straightening.
3. **Anticipation (2s):** Near-freeze. Bubu holds fully upright, trunk raised straight up, ears popped outward. Only a slow blink and faint breathing motion. The focus pebble remains perfectly still and pale.
4. **Payoff (3s):** In the first ~0.5s the focus pebble rapidly brightens from pale gray-blue to fully saturated turquoise and holds that glow. Simultaneously Bubu hops once, ears flap outward, trunk swings up and back into its raised hook position. The other two pebbles do not change. Over the remaining ~2.5s everything settles, the pebble's glow remaining steady.
5. **Loop (1s, generated at 2s minimum then trimmed):** Bubu eases from the Delight hop back into its calm Scene-1 resting pose and trunk curl; the focus pebble keeps its turquoise glow at a slightly softer intensity. End frame should closely match Scene 1's opening frame except for the now-glowing pebble.

## 5. Educational Objective

Introduces the action word "Point" and the result word "Shine," and teaches that paying close attention to one small thing (out of several similar things) is what makes it special — an early, gentle introduction to focus and selective attention.

## 6. Brand-Building Objective

Establishes Pebble Hill as a named, recurring location and dramatizes the Wonder Pebble mechanic's core rule — "the meadow remembers" — for the first time as the episode's entire subject, setting up every later Pebble Hill episode (05, 08, 17, 33, 59, 76, 79, 94, 99) to build on this origin point.
