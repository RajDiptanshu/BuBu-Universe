## 3. Video Prompts

Static, locked-off 9:16 camera throughout — no pans, tilts, zooms, or cuts. Image-to-video from each scene's keyframe; motion only.

1. **Hello (2s):** Gentle idle Wobble; edge pebble glows with a slow, soft steady pulse; the small bubble bobs very slightly on the puddle's surface. In the final frames, Bubu's body begins leaning forward and the trunk begins lifting toward the bubble.
2. **Build (4s):** The trunk performs three repeated forward "point" jabs toward the bubble, evenly spaced (~1.3s each); with each point the bubble drifts very slightly higher and grows very slightly larger, still pale. After the third point, the trunk lifts toward vertical, body straightening.
3. **Anticipation (2s):** Near-freeze. The bubble hangs motionless at its largest size, still pale. Bubu holds upright, trunk straight up, ears popped outward; only a slow blink and faint breathing motion.
4. **Payoff (3s):** In the first ~0.5s the bubble rapidly bursts into 2–3 fully saturated turquoise droplets that arc outward and land in the puddle, leaving a small turquoise ripple; the edge pebble's glow brightens in the same beat. Simultaneously Bubu hops once, ears flap outward, trunk swings up and back. Over the remaining ~2.5s the ripple and droplets settle.
5. **Loop (1s, generated at 2s minimum then trimmed):** Bubu eases from the Delight hop back into its calm Scene-1 resting pose and trunk curl; the puddle settles to calm pale blue-gray with no bubble, and the edge pebble returns to its steady soft glow. End frame should closely match Scene 1's opening frame except with no bubble visible.

## 5. Educational Objective

Introduces new vocabulary ("Bubble" and "Pop") and a new sequence shape — build through *watching and anticipating* rather than acting directly — while keeping the same 5-beat rhythm, broadening a toddler's vocabulary within a familiar setting.

## 6. Brand-Building Objective

Offers a variation within the Water Play "favorite thing" pillar by introducing a new transient object (the bubble) without adding a new location or character, while the Episode-01 edge pebble's brightening cameo continues to reinforce that The Puddle's Wonder Pebble persists and reacts across episodes.
