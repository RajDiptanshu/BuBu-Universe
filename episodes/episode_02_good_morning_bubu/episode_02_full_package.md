## 2. Image Prompts

> Prepend the **Shared Bubu Description** above to every prompt. Background for all 5 scenes: The Nook — a round dirt floor, curved-grass-blade walls, one oversized rounded leaf overhead as a roof (max 2 background set-pieces: the leaf-roof + one grass-wall shape), soft pastel tones, background saturation 0.20–0.35. One small pebble shape in the lower corner of frame, within the 3–5 object cap (Bubu + pebble + leaf-roof = 3). Vertical 9:16, Bubu 40–55% of frame height, eyes ~40% from top, center-left. No tusks, no wrinkles, no extra characters, no text, no watermark.

1. **Hello (Curious):** Bubu lying/sitting in a relaxed pose in The Nook, dim pale light, trunk in resting curl (0°, tip toward chest), pebble pale gray-blue (~0.25 sat) in the corner.
2. **Build (Focused):** Bubu sitting up, body slightly raised in a "stretch," trunk rotated ~90° pointing up toward a small light-gap in the leaf-roof; light-patch on the floor slightly brighter than Scene 1 but still pale (~0.30 sat); pebble unchanged.
3. **Anticipation (Surprise):** Bubu fully upright and still, trunk rotated ~180° straight up, ears popped outward; light-patch at its brightest pre-payoff pale state, holding still; pebble unchanged.
4. **Payoff (Delight):** Bubu mid-hop, one foot lifted, ears flapped outward, trunk rotated ~215–225° up and back toward the near ear; the light-patch fills the Nook floor with warm sunrise-cream (`#FFE3A8`, ~0.85 sat) — the single most saturated element in frame; the pebble now glows the same warm cream/gold.
5. **Loop (Curious, matches Scene 1):** Bubu back in its Scene-1 resting pose and trunk position (0°); light-patch faded back to pale/dim; pebble retains a faint warm-cream glow (slightly more saturated than Scene 1's pale gray-blue, signaling it is now a Wonder Pebble).

**Shared negative prompt:** tusks, wrinkles, skin texture, toenails, extra limbs, second character, multiple elephants, realistic elephant, photoreal, 3D render, text, watermark, signature, cropped, partial body, side/back view, more than 2 background set-pieces, cluttered background, oversaturated background, camera tilt/zoom, motion blur, more than 1 pebble.

## 3. Video Prompts

Static, locked-off 9:16 camera throughout — no pans, tilts, zooms, or cuts. Image-to-video from each scene's keyframe; motion only.

1. **Hello (2s):** Gentle idle Wobble (soft side-to-side sway, ears swaying with it). In the final frames, Bubu begins sitting up slightly, trunk beginning to lift toward the leaf-gap.
2. **Build (4s):** Bubu's trunk rises toward the leaf-gap and settles back down three times in a steady rhythm (~1.3s each), body rising slightly with each "stretch." The light-patch on the floor brightens very slightly with each repetition. Wobble continues between reps.
3. **Anticipation (2s):** Near-freeze. Bubu holds fully upright, trunk raised straight up, ears popped outward. Only a slow blink and faint breathing motion. Light-patch holds steady, no flicker.
4. **Payoff (3s):** The light-patch rapidly floods the floor with warm sunrise-cream light in the first ~0.5s; the corner pebble lights up the same color a beat later. Simultaneously Bubu hops once, ears flap outward, trunk swings up and back into its raised hook position. Over the remaining ~2.5s the light settles to a steady warm glow.
5. **Loop (1s, generated at 2s minimum then trimmed):** Bubu eases from the Delight hop back into its calm Scene-1 resting pose and trunk curl; the light-patch dims back toward its pale starting state while the corner pebble keeps a faint warm glow. End frame should closely match Scene 1's opening frame.

## 5. Educational Objective

Introduces the routine word "Morning" and the action word "Stretch," and models the first step of the Daily Wonder Cycle ("Wake up in The Nook") — teaching that every day, and every episode, begins the same calm, predictable way.

## 6. Brand-Building Objective

Establishes The Nook as Bubu's recognizable home base (Universe Bible Location #5) and gives the Wonder Pebble mechanic its second appearance in a completely different setting from Episode 01, proving the mechanic travels with Bubu anywhere — while reinforcing the Bubu Wobble, Ear-Perk & Point, and "Arre, Bubu!" in their canonical positions.
