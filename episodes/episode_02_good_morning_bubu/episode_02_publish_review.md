**Confirmed.** Direct frame comparison across the full 12.57s timeline:

- **t=7.9s (Scene 04 entry):** Massive golden rays covering the full upper-right of frame; Bubu in gasp pose; highest saturation and highest ambient light level of any sampled frame. This is the visual maximum.

- **t=11.4s (Scene 05 entry, ss=1.8 in source):** Golden rays present but centred and notably softer than Scene 04's flood; Bubu's smile is lighter and arms are partially lowered. No longer competing with Scene 04 for peak-energy reading.
- **t=11.9s (Scene 05 mid):** Eyes closing, rays dim — clearly the settle phase.

**Scene 04 is the single brightest and highest-energy scene.** ✓

## Scene 05 Verification — Calm Memory

**Confirmed.** The ss=1.8 entry point produces the intended reading:

- The first visible frame of Scene 05 (t≈11.32s) shows Bubu with a softer, smaller smile and arms at a settling position — the visible contrast against Scene 04's gasp pose and full ray-flood is immediate and clear.
- By t=11.5s (when text clears), Bubu's expression is transitioning toward closed-eye content.

**Scene 05 reads as calm warm memory — not a second payoff.** ✓

The remaining ambient warmth (soft golden glow still present in the background during Scene 05) is intentional: it communicates that the magic did happen, without re-energising the piece. A viewer reading Scene 05 in this cut experiences "Bubu sitting quietly with the memory of what just happened" rather than "a second celebration."
