# Episode 01 Full Package: "Bubu's Splash!"

**Pillar:** Water Play
**Runtime:** 12 seconds
**Hero color:** Turquoise (`#4FD1C5`)
**Key word:** "Splash"
**Why this episode first:** Highest retention potential (trunk-spray is Bubu's signature, brand-defining cause-and-effect move) and directly satisfies "prefer actions that use the trunk naturally."

| Beat | Time | Visual |

| 2 — Build | 0:02–0:06 | Bubu dips trunk-tip into the pond 3x (rhythmic, identical timing/size). Each dip creates one small ripple. "Focused" expression. |

- **Background music:** continuous, gentle, instrumental, light percussion (soft mallet/marimba) ticking in time with the Beat 2 trunk-dips; music swells slightly through Beat 3.
- **The one sound effect:** a single bright "splash/spray" sound, hits exactly on the Beat 4 payoff frame — synced to the water arc appearing.
- Beat 2's trunk-dips are marked by the **music's rhythm** (soft mallet hits), not by separate sound effects — keeping the "one SFX per episode" rule intact.
