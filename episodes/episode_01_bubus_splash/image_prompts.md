# Episode 01 — "Bubu's Splash" — Image Prompts

One image-generation prompt per scene. **Every prompt uses the approved Bubu reference sheet as the character-lock / image reference input** — supply that image alongside each prompt below at high identity strength (0.7-0.9) so Bubu's proportions, palette, ear shape, and trunk shape stay identical across all 5 frames.

> **Style note:** The approved reference image is a softly-shaded toon-3D illustration (gentle gradients, soft shadows) rather than a flat-outline vector style. The prompts below describe Bubu in those terms — pale gray-blue body, cream belly patch, large rounded ears with pink interior, short trunk with a curled hook tip, small rounded tail, blush cheeks, simple round eyes with white highlight, soft even lighting, no harsh shadows. Keep this rendering style identical across all 5 images.

## Scene 1 — Hello (Curious)

```
Using the approved Bubu reference image as character reference:

Bubu the baby elephant — pale gray-blue rounded body, cream belly patch,
large rounded ears with soft pink interior, short trunk with a gently curled
hook-shaped tip, small rounded tail, blush-pink cheeks, simple round dark
eyes with white highlight — three-quarter front view, standing pose, head
level, gentle "Curious" expression: soft raised eyebrows, gentle closed-curve
smile, ears relaxed and facing forward, trunk hanging in a relaxed curl near
the mouth.

Soft pastel meadow background (pale sky and ground, saturation 0.20-0.35,
maximum 2 simple background shapes). A small, calm, pale blue-gray puddle
(low saturation, ~0.25) sits in the lower-right third of the frame.

Vertical 9:16 composition, Bubu occupying 40-55% of frame height, eyes at
approximately 40% from top of frame, positioned center-left. Soft-shaded
toon-3D illustration style, gentle gradients, soft even lighting, no harsh
shadows. No tusks, no wrinkles, no extra characters, no text, no watermark.
```
